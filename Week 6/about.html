ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Caitlin!

I picked up my very first camera in 2016, and quickly encountered a constantly growing love of capturing simple, meaningful and beautiful moments in time. Photography is undeniably special. It enables you to capture a moment, tell a story, and provide a physical memory to look back on for years to come. It is an instrument of thought, passion and emotion. I love the way that through photography, you can truly capture the essence and humanity of a person, the emotion in an environment, and the simple candid moments of every-day life.
